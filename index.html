How to Add Your Signature in Gmail Guide
Takes about 1 minute
  1. Click Copy Signature on your signature card above.
  2. Open Gmail, then click the ⚙ gear icon at the top right and choose See all settings.
  3. In the General tab, scroll down to the Signature section.
  4. Click + Create new, type a name (e.g. TechNext), then click Create.
  5. Click inside the signature box and paste with Ctrl + V (⌘ Cmd + V on Mac).
  6. Under Signature defaults, select your new signature for For new emails and On reply/forward.
  7. Scroll to the very bottom and click Save Changes.
💡 The logo and icons load from the web automatically — just paste, don't re-upload any images.
How to Change Your Profile Picture Guide
Your photo shows on every email you send
  1. Open Gmail and click your profile photo in the top-right circle.
  2. Click the camera icon on your photo, or click Manage your Google Account.
  3. Go to the Personal info tab, then click your photo under "Profile picture".
  4. Click Add profile picture (or Change) and upload your image from your computer.
  5. Crop or reposition the image, then click Save as profile picture.
💡 Profile pictures display as a still image — animated GIFs won't move. It can take a few minutes to update everywhere.
